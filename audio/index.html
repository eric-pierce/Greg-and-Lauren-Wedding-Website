THINGS TO DO

We know that everyone will be traveling for our wedding so we want you to make a vacation for yourself while you're here! Here are some of our favorite things to do here on Sanibel.

GOLFING AT THE DUNES

The ceremony will be held at the Dunes Resort, which is also home to one of the Island's best golf courses.

DING DARLING REFUGE

Ding Darling's 5,200 acre refuge is the largest refuge on the island. Bike or drive the refuge trails!

TARPON BAY KAYAKING

Tarpon Bay is home to a wonderful kayaking trail amongst the mangroves.

SANIBEL BEACHES

Sanibel Island has incredible natural beaches, with some of the best shell hunting in the world!

PERIWINKLE PLACE

Some of the best shopping on the Island, lots of different boutiques.

THE MUCKY DUCK

Located on Captiva Island a short drive away, is our favorite beachside english style pub!
